# Epic 6: Knowledge & Learning

## Problem Statement

Magellan's knowledge graph is built from internal documents, but there's no
capability to pull in external context from the web. Market positioning,
competitor analysis, and public documentation about the tech stack require
manual research outside Magellan.

## Desired Outcome

Magellan can supplement its document-based knowledge graph with web research
for market/competitive context, maintaining the same source attribution
standards.

## Features

- [F29: Deep Research with Source Attribution](../features/29-deep-research.md)

## Inspired By

- ECC: deep-research skill with multi-source web research and citations

## Magellan Advantage

Magellan can integrate web research directly into the knowledge graph with
full source tracing -- turning market context into first-class knowledge
alongside internal documentation.
